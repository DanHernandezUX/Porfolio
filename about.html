Hello. I'm Dan

I’m a UX designer specializing in experience design and interaction. I’ve spent the better part of 5 years designing digital experiences for B2B and B2C audiences in a variety of industries, carefully crafting simplified experiences.

Most recently, I've worked in the Mobility-as-a-Service (MaaS) industry designing both web and native mobile app experiences. I did my best work mapping user flows, wireframing, and conducting user interviews.

I'm interested in continuing to push my knowledge of designing mobile experiences with products that can be found in the iOS app or Google Play Stores.
